Frequently Asked Roofing Questions

What types of roofs can I purchase?

We offer asphalt shingles, metal roofing, tile, and eco-friendly options, customized to your home’s style and budget.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most roof replacements take 1-3 days, depending on the roof size and weather conditions. We’ll provide a timeline during your quote.

Can you repair small roof leaks?

Yes, our expert team specializes in detecting and fixing leaks quickly, preventing further damage to your home.

What are the benefits of solar installation?

Solar panels reduce energy bills, increase home value, and support eco-friendly living. We handle permits and installation seamlessly.

How does roof waterproofing work?

Our waterproofing solutions use advanced sealants and membranes to protect your roof from water damage, extending its lifespan.
